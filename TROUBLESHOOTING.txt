ISSUE 2: ADMIN PAGE EMPTY
------------------------
You asked specifically **NOT** to use a Server (Node.js). I built this using "Local Storage".
* **Local Storage** means data is saved **Inside the Browser** of the device you are using.
* If you book on your **Phone**, the data is saved on your **Phone**.
* Your **Computer** cannot see what is inside your Phone's storage.
* That is why the Admin Page on your Computer is empty.
